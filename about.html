Who We Are

About LIT

LIT isn't trying to be everything to everyone. It's a neighborhood bar in the truest sense. A place where you can walk in any night of the week and feel at home. Where the bartender knows your order, your dog is welcome on the patio, and there's always something happening without the pretense.

Sitting right in the heart of Wilton Drive, LIT is part of the daily rhythm of Wilton Manors. Whether it's poker on Tuesday, Drag Race on Thursday, bingo on Sunday, or just a cold drink after a long day, this is where the neighborhood comes together.

We're a sports bar, a hangout, a weekend party, and a Sunday brunch spot. We're whatever you need us to be, whenever you need it.
